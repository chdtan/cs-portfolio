About me

Hello! My name is Chitra! I'm a Web Developer!

I am a firm believer in continuous learning. I am always eager to improve my skills and toolbox. I also believe in helping people and society in real and tangible ways. This led me to becoming a Web Developer which has allowed me to build tools and applications that have an immediate impact for my clients. I wish to work towards gaining a better understanding of the reaches of technology.

I am a Graduate of HackerYou's Web Development Immersive Program. I also hold a Bachelor of Arts from Rutgers University, New Jersey as well as a Paralegal Certificate from Fairlegh Dickinson Univerity, New Jersey.
